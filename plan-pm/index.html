Quick FAQ

How do I set up the app after first launch?

To see your schedule, go to Settings and choose: Faculty, Program, Year, Specialization, and Study Mode. The schedule will be downloaded automatically.

I can see classes for all groups in my year. How can I fix that?

This is the key Plan PM feature. Open "Group selection" and select only the groups you belong to (for example, L01 for labs or A02 for auditoriums). The app will hide classes for other groups and clean up your view.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. After the first schedule download, it is stored on your phone. You can check your timetable offline (for example, in buildings with weak signal). Connect to the internet from time to time to fetch schedule updates.

Why is my schedule empty?

Make sure that:

  • You have an internet connection (for first-time setup).
  • You selected the correct settings data (Faculty/Program).
  • If it is a weekend or holiday, check another day of the week.
Is dark mode available?

Yes. The app automatically follows your phone system appearance. If dark mode is enabled on your device, Plan PM will use dark mode too.

Where do the in-app news updates come from?

News is fetched directly from official Maritime University of Szczecin communication channels, so you stay up to date with university life.
