Our Philosophy

We will annoy you until it's done. You cannot swipe away the problem. The only way to silence overdue spam is to open the app and complete the task.

Key Features

  • Relentless Overdue Spam: once a deadline passes, expect a notification onslaught so tasks stop slipping through.
  • Smart Escalation Engine: starts at every minute, then dynamically adjusts intervals (exponential backoff) to keep pressure on for days without crushing battery life.
  • Precision Pre-Reminders: set exact reminders before deadlines. We are a sniper early and a spam bus when you are late.
  • Ultra-Minimalist Design: clean, distraction-free interface with full dark mode support.
  • Works Offline: notifications are scheduled directly on your device. No cloud delays, no excuses.
