Quick FAQ

What does UntilDone do?

UntilDone helps you stay productive by reminding you about upcoming tasks and continuing notifications when tasks are overdue until they are completed.

How do reminder notifications work?

The app sends reminder notifications for tasks with deadlines. If a task is overdue, it keeps notifying you and dynamically adjusts notification intervals based on how late the task is.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Core task management and reminder functionality work offline.

What internet data does the app use?

The app uses internet access only to connect to our database with scraped data from plany.am.szczecin.pl.

Do you collect or log user data?

No. We do not store or log user data.

Which languages are supported?

UntilDone currently supports French, English, Spanish, German, Italian, and Polish.
